# 📊 Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data using Excel and Power BI to uncover key business insights, track performance metrics, and support data-driven decision-making. It focuses on sales trends, customer behavior, and overall business performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance  
- Identify top-performing products and customer segments  
- Track revenue and profit trends  
- Improve business decision-making through insights  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- Power BI (Dashboard Development, DAX)
- Excel (Pivot Tables, Data Analysis)

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Identified high-performing product categories driving revenue  
- Analyzed profit vs sales trends to evaluate business efficiency  
- Segmented customers based on purchasing behavior  
- Evaluated customer feedback and ratings for improvement  

---

## 📈 Business Impact
- Improved visibility into sales and profitability  
- Enabled better decision-making using dashboards  
- Identified growth opportunities and performance gaps
